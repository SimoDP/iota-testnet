🎯 Hackathon MVP – start simple

Quick reminder for everyone 👇
For the hackathon, the goal is not to build a complete product, but a clear and focused MVP.

What we recommend:

1)start from one clear problem

2)design one core feature that solves it

3)keep everything simple, light, and testable

A good MVP shows:

1)clarity of the idea

2)a solid use case

3)a realistic implementation in the given time

Extra features can always go into a future roadmap.

Focus on the core first 🚀

If you have doubts on scope, concept, or how to simplify your idea, feel free to ask here or book a call with the MasterZ team.

MasterZ × IOTA Hackathon

⚒️ Building phase – Evaluation rules-Remind

To be eligible for evaluation, your project must meet these requirements:

Real-world problem
The project must address a concrete, real-world use case.

Built on IOTA Layer 1
The core logic must be developed directly on IOTA Testnet/Mainnet (IOTA L1-Move)

Use of IOTA services
The solution must integrate at least one of the following:

IOTA Digital Identity

IOTA Notarization

IOTA Hierarchies  (Anything from the trust framework) https://docs.iota.org/developer/iota-trust-framework

or

IOTA Tokenization

Working MVP
Deliver a functional MVP that clearly demonstrates the core value of the solution.

The focus is on feasibility, clarity, and real adoption potential, not on feature completeness.
